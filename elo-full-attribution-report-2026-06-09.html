Key Finding

ELOs capture some activity from all four gradient surfaces, but the level of attribution varies significantly:

  • Agentforce (L1) — Full attribution with BOT_ID, BOT_SESSION_ID, and PLANNER_ID across multiple ELO types
  • Cursor/sf-cli (L2) — Full attribution via CLIENT_NAME="sfdx toolbelt" in RestApi ELO
  • sf-dashboard (L3) — Partial: Login shows "node" browser, but query-level attribution missing
  • agentforce-today (L4) — Partial: MCP endpoints visible, but SOQL queries routed through MCP not captured
